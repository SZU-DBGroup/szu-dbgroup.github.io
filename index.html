Welcome to the Database Group in Shenzhen University (SZU).

About the Database Group

This database group is affiliated with Big Data Institude (led by Prof. (Joshua) Zhexue Huang) in Shenzhen University. Currently, we have the following directions.

  • Efficient Algorithms for Geographic Information Systems

  • Efficient Algorithms for Large-scale Data Visualization

  • Efficient Query Processing for Spatial/Graph Databases

  • Distributed Algorithms for Spatial/Graph Databases

  • Development of Efficient Software Packages and System Prototypes

    • In this group, we have published many papers in top-tier conferences and journals in the database/data management field, including SIGMOD, PVLDB, ICDE, TODS, VLDBJ, and TKDE. If you are interested in joining us, please contact us via these two email addresses, which are (1) edisonchan2013928@gmail.com (Prof. Tsz Nam Chan) and (2) dingming.wu@gmail.com (Dr. Dingming Wu).     Recruitment
    Our database group is now hiring an assistant professor and an associate researcher (副研究员). Interested applicants (1) should have strong academic background in database research or other related fields (e.g., publish papers in prestigious conferences and journals (e.g., CCF: A and CSranking)), (2) must show independent research skills and (3) must show strong presentation skills. Applicants who successfully join this research group are expected to work with us and other members in Big Data Institute.
